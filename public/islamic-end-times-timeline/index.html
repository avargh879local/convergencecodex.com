This page gives a beginner sequence for major Islamic end-times figures. Exact ordering can vary across scholars and narrations, so this is a comparison map rather than a fatwa.

Why A Timeline Helps

The most important comparison question is not simply "who looks like whom?" It is "who performs which role, in what order, and who defeats whom?" The Islamic sequence makes the Dajjal problem especially important for the Mahdi-Antichrist thesis.

Quick Questions

Does every Islamic scholar give the same end-times sequence?

No. The broad figures are well known, but exact ordering and interpretation can vary across hadith reports, scholars, and sectarian traditions.

Why does Isa matter in the Islamic timeline?

Isa ibn Maryam descends near the end in major Islamic traditions, aligns with the Muslim community, and kills Dajjal.
