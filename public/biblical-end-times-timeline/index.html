Why A Timeline Helps

The Mahdi-Antichrist question is not only about traits. It is about sequence. Who rises first? Who validates whom? Who is defeated by whom? Timelines make the agreements and contradictions easier to see.

Quick Questions

Is this timeline accepted by every Christian tradition?

No. This page presents a simplified futurist timeline used for comparison. Other Christian traditions read Daniel, Matthew, Thessalonians, and Revelation differently.

Why compare timelines instead of only traits?

Timelines show who rises, who endorses whom, and who defeats whom. Those sequence questions are crucial in Mahdi, Dajjal, Antichrist, and False Prophet comparisons.
