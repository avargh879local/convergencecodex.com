Sources For The Study

This page collects the main texts and debate voices used by the Convergence Codex. It prioritizes primary sources, then clearly separates later interpretation from scripture.

Biblical Texts

  • Daniel 7 - final oppressive ruler, persecution of the saints, divine judgment.
  • Daniel 9:24-27 - covenant and abomination language used in many futurist readings.
  • Matthew 24:15-31 - Jesus' Olivet Discourse and abomination language.
  • 1 John 2:18; 2 John 7 - direct use of "antichrist" terminology.
  • 2 Thessalonians 2:1-12 - man of lawlessness and self-exaltation language.
  • Revelation 13 - Beast, second beast, signs, and mark.
  • Revelation 19-20 - defeat of Beast and False Prophet; Gog and Magog.
  • Ezekiel 38-39 - Gog of Magog and divine intervention.

Islamic Texts

  • Quran 18:94-99 - Ya'juj and Ma'juj and the barrier narrative.
  • Quran 21:96 - Ya'juj and Ma'juj are let loose near the end.
  • Quran 43:61 - Jesus as a sign connected with the Hour in many interpretations.
  • Sahih Muslim 2933-2937 range - major Dajjal, Isa, and Ya'juj/Ma'juj reports.
  • Sahih Muslim 2897 - descent of the son of Mary while a leader from the Muslim community is present.
  • Sunan Abi Dawud, Jami al-Tirmidhi, Sunan Ibn Majah, Musnad Ahmad - Mahdi-related reports with varying gradings.
  • Nuaym ibn Hammad, Kitab al-Fitan - influential apocalyptic material, often requiring extra caution because some reports are weak.

Source Rule

The site should never treat a viral claim as settled. Every major comparison needs a primary text, a clearly named interpretation, and a note about strength or weakness.
