Privacy Policy

This site is currently a static research website. It does not have accounts, comments, checkout, or a contact form.

Information We Collect

The Convergence Codex does not directly collect personal information through forms or user accounts. Basic server and security logs may be processed by Cloudflare because the site is hosted through Cloudflare Pages and protected by Cloudflare network services.

Cookies And Analytics

At this stage, the site does not place advertising cookies or run Google Analytics. If analytics, newsletter forms, or advertising are added later, this page should be updated before those tools go live.

Advertising

The site may apply for advertising in the future. If Google AdSense or another ad network is added, the privacy policy will be updated to disclose cookies, ad personalization, and visitor choices before ad code is deployed.

Contact

A public correction/contact channel will be added before monetization. Until then, the site should be treated as a read-only public research archive.
